Disruption Frequency (DF)
\( DF = \frac{N_{disruptions}}{T} \)
Plot by company as bar chart
Average Recovery Time (ART)
\( ART = \frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^N (t^{rec}_i-t^{event}_i) \)
Plot as histogram
High-Impact Disruption Rate (HDR)
\( HDR = \frac{N_{high\,impact}}{N_{disruptions}}\times100\% \)
Share of disruptions classified as "high severity"
Total Downtime (TD)
\( TD = \sum_{i=1}^{N}(t^{rec}_i-t^{event}_i) \)
Plot as histogram
Regional Risk Concentration (RRC)
\( RRC_r = \frac{N_{disruptions, r}}{N_{disruptions, total}} \)
Plot as heatmap
